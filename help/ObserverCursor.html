Observer Cursor Tab

The cursor tab shows information about the object currently focussed by the mouse pointer.

  • In the tractory editor of the timeline frame, the timeline position indicated by the horizontal mouse coordiate is shown along with the trajectory's X,Y position at this point. When selecting transmitters in the left transmitter track column, the observer swithes to the transmitter tab. When the timeline is modified using the timeline axis, the observer switches to the timeline tab.
  • On the surface pane when one of the tools is active, the current mouse coordinates are shown. When the surface pointer tool is used and receivers are selected, the observer switches to the receiver tab. When using the surface pencil or surface fork tool, the cursor tab shows the sensitivity of the most prominent receivers and the energy sum of all receivers.
  • In the receiver's sensitivity editor, the sensitivity is shown with respect to the distance and angle given by the current mouse coordinates.
