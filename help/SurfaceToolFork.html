Fork Surface Tool

The tuning fork tool (keyboard F6) works very similar to the pencil tool, with two important differences:

  • no trajectories are written. this tool is therefore used to rehearse movements prior to the final recording, or to query the sensitivities at certain spots of the surface.
  • the transport is not automatically started and stopped if not already running.

Just like the pencil tool, since movements are transmitted as 'buffers' to the realtime plug-in, you while notice a slight delay of the audible movement of sound which corresponds to the realtime sense buffer size.
