Transport Tools

The transport tools control realtime playback of the session. From left to right you see the Rewind, Stop, Play, Fast Forward button, the Loop and the Realtime toggle. Keyboard shortcuts are Space for Play/Stop, Numpad-/ for Loop and Numpad-* for Realtime toggle. Rewind and Fast Forward will have shortcuts in the next version.

Even without having a synthesis programme running in the background (when the Realtime Plug-In is disabled), you can use the transport to view the transmitter movements in realtime or to record movements using the pencil surface tool. Hint: you can temporarily alter the playback speed by changing the sense data sampling rate in the preference's session tab.

Loop playback: the loop playback uses the current timeline selection and can be altered using the timeline axis, the timeline pointer tool or the observer time tab.
