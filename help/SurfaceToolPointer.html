Pointer Surface Tool

The pointer tool (keyboard F1) is used to select or deselect and drag receivers on the surface. Single receivers can be selected by clicking on their anchor point (the red crosshair labelled with the receiver's name). Shift+Click allows multiple selections. Clicking outside a receiver on the surface will deselect all receivers. The selected receivers are shown with a blue shading. Click+Drag will move a single or multiple receivers.

Double clicking on the surface will create a new receiver at the current mouse position. As of version 0.72, this is a SigmaReceiver; to create other classes of receivers, use the menu item File->Insert->New Receivers.

Pressing Delete or selecting an item from the Edit menu will affect all currently selected receivers. Numeric editing and renaming is performed in the receiver tab of the observer palette. Double clicking on a receiver's anchor point will open the receiver's sensitity editor.

When the pointer tool is active and the surface frame is focussed, pressing Meta+A will select all receivers.

The pointer tool does not affect transmitters. They can be selected or deselected in their corresponding tracks in the timeline frame.
