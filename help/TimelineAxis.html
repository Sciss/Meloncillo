Timeline Axis

The timelime axis displays a ruler scaled and positioned such that the labels correspond to the time frame displayed in the trajectory tracks. The units are seconds. The current timeline position used for playback and clipboard pasting is indicated by a vertical red bar which can be shifted by clicking and dragging in the timeline axis. The currently active timeline selection (gray or blue shaded) used for delete, cut and copy operations and loop playback can be alterted by Shift+Click in the timeline axis. Alt+Click will erase the timeline selection.

The numeric timeline position can be altered in the transport palette. The numeric timeline selection can be altered in the time tab of the observer palette. To quickly return to the session beginning, press Return when the timeline frame is focussed.

A 'bird's view' of the timeline position and selection is shown in the timeline scrollbar.

Often, it will be more convenient, to use the timeline's pointer to tool to set the timeline position and selected span.
