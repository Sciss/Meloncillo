Main Progress Bar

Some processes might take a few seconds or even longer to finish. These processes run in an asynchronous thread and report their progress by animating this progress bar. For longer processes such as bouncing to disk, an estimation of the remaining time is displayed as HH:MM:SS, but due to the linear extrapolation don't expect this to be too accurate.

When a process is finished, the bar turns green in case of success and red in case of failure.
