Surface Frame

The surface frame can be opened from the window menu (Meta+Numpad-*). The right side shows the surface, which the spatial setting of the session with optional display of receiver sensitivities and transmitter trajectories. It is what is called the "canvas" in video editing software such as final cut. On the top border you see the surface toolbar controlling the modes of editing on the surface.

The surface is separated from two object tables on the left margin of the window. These tables are hidden by default and can be made visible by clicking on the vertical split bar of the window. The tables show a list of all receivers and groups in the session. The selection of groups affects which objects are shown on the surface and in the timeline frame.
