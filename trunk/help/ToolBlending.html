Blending Mode

When the blending mode is activated (keyboard Caps-Lock), some trajectory editing operations are blended in and out at the operation's punch in and out points.

The amount of blending is specified in a separate dialog which will be opened when you Alt+Click on the blending mode icon. Suppose a blending of 0.5 sec. is activated. Drawing a line using the line tool when the timeline selection is (4.0 sec. ... 6.0 sec.) will create a linear fade-in from 4.0 sec. to 4.5 sec. and a linear fade-out from 5.5 sec. to 6.0 sec.

Blending works with the surface tools line, curve, arc and pencil, affects direct drawing to the trajectory timeline tracks and applying a trajectory filter. In a future version, blending will also apply to cut and paste clipboard operations and offer a polar (instead of cartesian) mode.
