Pencil Timeline Tool

The pencil tool (keyboard F3) is used to draw directly into the trajectory tracks. When blending is active, the beginning and ending of a draw gesture is faded in and out to prevent sudden jumps. You will usually want to edit trajectories on the surface though, using the surface pencil tool for example. For increased vertical resolution, when drawing directly in the trajectory tracks, select the tracks first, then press Ctrl+Cursor Down.
