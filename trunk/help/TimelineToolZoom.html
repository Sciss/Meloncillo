Zoom Timeline Tool

The zoom tool (keyboard F4) is used to increase or decrease the horizontal (time) resolution of the timeline frame. Alternatively press Ctrl+Cursor Left/Right. To zoom in, Click+Drag anywhere on the trajectory pane. To zoom out, Alt+Click anywhere on the trajectory pane.

Note that this tool does not influence vertical zoom. To increase or decrease vertical zoom, select the tracks first, then press Ctrl+Cursor Up/Down.
