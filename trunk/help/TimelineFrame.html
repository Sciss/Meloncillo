Timeline Frame

The timeline frame can be opened from the window menu (Meta+Numpad-=). It resembles the edit window in protools or the arrange window in logic pro, since it shows a timeline axis with time advancing from left to right, a list of tracks corresponding to the trajectories of the transmitters. on the bottom, the timeline scrollbar allows to navigate in the timeline. operations on the tracks are determined by the choice of a tool from the timeline toolbar on the top margin.
